Southport, Maine

We hope you can join us in celebrating!

Summertime in the midcoast region of Maine is beautiful, and it's where Emma and Jake first met while attending Bowdoin College in Brunswick. We're tying the knot a bit up the coast from there, just outside of Boothbay Harbor.



Venue: Newagen Seaside Inn

We'll be hosting all of the weekend's events here, less than a 15 minute drive outside of Boothbay Harbor. The inn is located astride Cape Newagen on the southern end of Southport Island.

Getting here from Portland is straightforward, and 90 minutes without traffic. Follow I-295 to Brunswick, then US Route 1 to Edgecomb, turning onto local route 27. If you follow 27 it will skirt Boothbay Harbor, cross onto Southport, and end at Newagen.

Accomodation is available on site, and we have room blocks for less expensive options on Southport (Ocean Gate Resort) and back in town (Boothbay Harbor Inn, more affordable option). There are also a wide variety of Airbnbs available in Boothbay Harbor, in the adjacent town of Boothbay, and along Route 1.
